> Lower cost with abundant materials
> No rare metals, limiting supply chain risks and mining impacts
> No cycling degradation, consistent performance over decades

Our Product

Unlocking the potential of thermal storage

Thermal batteries are inherently more complex than electrochemical batteries: they are charged by HVAC, governed by complex thermodynamics, and coupled to thermal processes.

ThermoShift accurately models and optimizes the operation of these complex thermal systems in real time, across electricity markets, HVAC performance, and local self-consumption.
